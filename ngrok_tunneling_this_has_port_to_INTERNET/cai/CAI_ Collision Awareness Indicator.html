Active Risk · Turku intersections
The Problem

The right-hook blind spot

A car drives in the right lane and turns right on green. A cyclist in the bike lane approaches from behind, also on green, going straight through the crossing.

As the car turns, the driver checks left for oncoming traffic. The cyclist comes from the right-rear blind spot. The turn cuts directly across the bike lane.

Both following every rule. The intersection geometry creates the collision. CAI breaks that chain before it starts.

The Solution

A device. A system. One mission.

CAI is both an embedded IoT unit on existing infrastructure and a trained AI model that understands collision probability in real time.

Hardware · Embedded Device

The CAI Unit

A compact weatherproof module mounted to the top of the existing traffic light pole — powered directly from the pole's electrical supply. No new poles, no roadworks.

RGB camera — day visionFull colour processing in daylight and overcast Finnish conditions.
Infrared camera — night visionActive IR sees through darkness, fog, sleet and snow.
360° wide-angle field of viewCovers the bike lane approach and full crossing ahead.
Powered by the traffic lightTaps the existing pole supply. Zero additional infrastructure cost.
High-intensity warning flood lightIlluminates the crossing from above when risk is detected.
Software · Collision Prediction Model

The AI System

A trained ML model running on-device. Not rule-based — it learns the right-hook scenario and predicts collision probability continuously in real time.

Trained on the right-hook scenarioSpecifically modelled for Finnish layout — bike lane right of road, car turning across it.
Live video inferenceDetects object class, speed and direction vector in real time.
Collision probability scoreComputes time-to-conflict for both parties. Sub-second trigger when risk exceeds threshold.
Edge computing — fully embeddedRuns entirely on-device. No cloud, no latency, fully offline.
Remotely updatableModel retrained on new intersection data as fleet grows.
🔒

Privacy by design

Video is processed live and deleted immediately — nothing is stored. In the event of a detected collision, a short clip is automatically encrypted and held locally until requested by authorities, then permanently deleted. No surveillance. No data sales. No third-party access.

How It Works

Three steps. Under one second.

The full cycle from detection to warning happens faster than a human reaction time.

01

Detect

The 360° camera watches the bike lane approach — day via RGB, night via infrared. Every object, speed and direction tracked live.

02

Predict

The on-device AI model computes a collision probability score. If cyclist and car are converging within the critical window, the system fires.

03

Warn

A bright light floods the crossing from the pole top. Both parties warned at once. No app needed.
