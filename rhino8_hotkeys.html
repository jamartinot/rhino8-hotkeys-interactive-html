Rhino 8: Master Command Tree & Workflow Guide

Complete chronological reference including sub-commands and mouse actions

Part 1: UI, Camera & The Gumball00:00

  • Camera Controls
    • Drag Orbit View
    • Shift + Drag Pan View
    • Ctrl + Drag (or Scroll) Smooth Zoom
    • Zs Zoom Selected (centers view on object)
  • Viewport Management
    • Dbl-Click View Name Toggle max/4-view
    • Click View Name Change display (Shaded, etc)
    • F7 Toggle visibility of the Grid
  • Gumball Tool (Widget for transforms)
    • Drag Arrows Move object along axis
      • + Tap Alt Duplicates object (Copy)
    • Drag Arcs Rotate object
      • + Hold Shift Constrains to Ortho (90° snaps)
    • Drag Squares 1D Scale (stretches 1 axis)
      • + Hold Shift Uniform 3D Scale
    • Modify Gumball
      • Dbl-Click handle Move Gumball only
      • Click Menu icon Toggle "Auto Reset"
  • Visibility & Selection
    • L-R Drag Window select (fully enclosed)
    • R-L Drag Crossing select (anything touched)
    • Shift + Click Add to selection
    • Ctrl + Click Remove from selection
    • Invert + Hide / Lock Isolate/Lock items
  • Precise Moving
    • Move Move from Pt A to Pt B via Snaps
    • Orient3Pt Align 3 source points ➔ 3 target points

Part 2 & 3: Geometry & Sub-Selection00:16

  • The Geometry Hierarchy
    • Join Combines touching curves/surfaces
    • Explode Breaks Polysurfaces into parts
    • ExtrudeCrv Pulls 2D lines into 3D geometry
    • Cap Closes flat holes to create a solid
    • PlanarSrf Fills flat 2D curve with a surface
    • DupBorder Extracts the outline of a surface
  • Control Points & Sub-Select
    • F10 (or PointsOn) Show editable points
    • F11 (or PointsOff) Hide editable points
    • Ctrl + Shift + Click Select 1 face/edge of a solid
    • ConvertExtrusion Fixes simple extrusions for F10
  • NURBS vs Meshes vs SubD
    • NURBS Smooth, exact math geometry (CAD default)
      • Rebuild Changes Point Count & Degree
    • Meshes Polygonal geometry (Vertices/Edges)
    • SubD Organic, clay-like geometry
      • Tab Toggle Smooth/Box mode
      • Ctrl + Gumball Drag Extrudes SubD face
      • Subdivide Breaks 1 SubD face into 4

Part 4, 5 & 6: Layers, Submenus & 2D to 3D Forms00:36

  • Layer Management
    • Tab Press while renaming to quickly create next layer
    • Click Layer ➔ Change Object Layer
    • Sub-layers Drag & Drop. Prefix with "Z_" to sort to bottom.
  • Curve to 3D Generators
    • Loft Surface bridging multiple curves
      • Options: Normal, Straight Sections, Align Seams
    • Revolve Spins curve around axis (Full Circle)
    • Pipe Turns curve into a tube (Cap: Flat/Round)
    • Sweep1 Extrudes shape along 1 guide rail
    • Sweep2 Extrudes shape bridging 2 guide rails
  • 2D Editing & Toolbar Submenus
    • Click Corner Triangle Expands nested toolbars
    • Line ➔ Perpendicular/Tangent to 2 Curves
    • Offset Expands/shrinks a curve evenly
    • CurveBoolean Rapid trim: Click inside regions to keep
    • FilletCorners Rounds all 2D corners at once
    • BlendCrv Smoothly connects 2 curves (G3/G4)
    • InsertControlPoint Adds editable points

Part 7 & 8: Snaps, Aids & CPlanes01:06

  • Nudge (Micro-movement)
    • Arrows Normal Nudge (0.2 units)
    • Ctrl + Arrows Small Nudge (0.05 units)
    • Shift + Arrows Large Nudge
    • PageUp / PageDown Vertical Nudge (Z-axis)
  • Snaps & Constraints
    • Shift Temporarily toggles Ortho (45°/90°)
    • Alt Temporarily toggles Object Snaps on/off
    • Planar Snap Keeps drawing flat to first clicked elevation
    • Osnap Types: End, Near, Pt, Mid, Cen, Int, Quad, Knot
  • Construction Planes (CPlanes)
    • Top=XY, Front=ZX, Right=ZY. Commands act relative to view.
    • Trim ➔ Apparent Intersections = Yes (Allows cutting visually overlapping curves from specific views)
    • Project Throws curve onto 3D surface (View dependent)
    • ProjectToCPlane Flattens 3D curve absolutely flat

Part 9 & 10: Advanced Solid Edits01:20

  • Shortcuts & Customization
    • Space / Enter / Click Repeats last cmd
    • Click Opens Quick Menu
    • Ctrl + Drag Saves toolbar button to MMB Menu
    • SelCrv, SelSrf, SelLast, SelDup
  • Splitting, Scaling & Booleans
    • Rotate3D Rotates around a custom drawn axis line
    • Scale1D / Scale2D Stretches 1 or 2 axes via points
    • InterpCrvOnSrf Draws curve snapped directly on surface
    • Split vs Trim Split leaves pieces; Trim deletes excess
    • Booleans (Requires closed solids)
      • BooleanUnion Fuses overlaps together
      • BooleanDifference Carves holes
      • BooleanIntersection Keeps only the overlap
    • FilletEdge / ChamferEdge Rounds/bevels solid edges

Part 11: Arrays, Flow & Cage Deformations01:49

  • Repeating Geometry (Arrays)
    • ArrayLinear / Array / ArrayPolar Duplicates in rows, 3D grids, or circular patterns
    • ArrayCrvOnSrf / ArraySrf Patterns objects onto specific targeted surface coordinates
  • Global Flow & History
    • FlowAlongSrf Warps a flat object to wrap identically over an organic surface
    • History (Record=Yes, Lock=Yes). Modifying the parent curve automatically updates the generated child geometry.
  • Deformations
    • Twist / Bend / Taper Applies global, sweeping bending or twisting forces along an axis
    • CageEdit Wraps objects in an editable point-box (BoundingBox > World > 4,4,4) to organically squish/stretch groupings
